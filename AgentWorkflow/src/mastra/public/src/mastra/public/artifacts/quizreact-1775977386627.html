TCP: Transmission Control Protocol

💡 Key Insight: TCP guarantees delivery through acknowledgments—like certified mail for data packets!

Connection-Oriented Protocol
🎓 Teaching Tip: Use the analogy of a phone call (TCP) vs. shouting across a room (UDP). Emphasize that the three-way handshake is like saying 'Hello? Can you hear me? Yes, I can hear you!' before starting a conversation. When discussing the state diagram, walk through a complete connection lifecycle step-by-step, pausing at ESTABLISHED to highlight that this is where actual data transfer occurs.
